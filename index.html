Let's know something about her journey

At the age of 16, Jiya started "MX TAKATAK" as an artist (Jiya The Coolartist). Her first video was uploaded on August 15, 2020, recorded by her brother. Initially, she didn't know how to edit, so she re-recorded it on trending songs. Watch here.

She learned oil pastels after 7 months and explored different colors by watching other artists like Aryan Verma. Her content stands out because she focuses on unique trending video concepts.

Fun Fact: On 26 January 2022, Jiya made 1000 smallest drawings in a single day. Watch here.

She pays attention to details that many artists miss, for example in her Kedarnath drawing: View Kedarnath Drawing.
