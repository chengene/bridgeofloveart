Who Am I?

Jiya the Coolartist is a digital and traditional artist known for creating intricate and engaging art videos on YouTube. Her work seamlessly blends traditional mediums with digital art forms, and she is especially celebrated for producing vibrant art videos set to rap music — making every creation a full sensory experience.

One of Jiya's most unique traits is her ability to create a high volume of small, detailed drawings in a single day — showcasing both dedication and creative stamina that inspires artists of all skill levels.

“Art is not what you see, but what you make others see.”

About This Project

This portfolio website — Jiya The Coolartist — serves as a digital gallery and learning platform built to showcase different art styles and provide helpful resources for beginners stepping into the world of art.

  • 🖼️ Curated art galleries across five unique styles
  • 📺 YouTube video integration for process walkthroughs
  • 💌 A “Get in Touch” section for commissions & collaborations
  • 🎓 Beginner-friendly layout to inspire new artists
